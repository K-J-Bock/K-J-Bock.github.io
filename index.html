Bubble Balance - Keep the Bubble in the Perfect Size!

About the Game:

Get ready for the ultimate bubble pop challenge! In Bubble Balance, your task is to keep a rising bubble in the perfect size while skillfully avoiding collisions with obstacles along the way. With its simple controls and intuitive gameplay, Bubble Balance is easy to pick up for players of all ages, yet it offers a satisfying challenge that will test your skills and reflexes.
